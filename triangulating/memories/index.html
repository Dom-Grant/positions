I see a lot of conceptual similarity between my work taking discarded, forgotten, or unwanted artefacts and recontextualising them to create a small part of a larger, crafted narrative and the way How To with John Wilson uses footage from unknowing participants found in public life as short companion clips to the larger narrated story presented throughout an episode. For example, at the start of this episode John talks about how looking up can reveal danger (footage of someone abseiling to clean windows), scandal (footage of people having sex by a high up hotel window), and suspense (footage of a dog trapped on a ledge outside an apartment).
